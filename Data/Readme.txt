=========================
DATA STRUCTURE/REPOSITORY
=========================

The Data folder contains initial datasets used in our analysis. Below are our datasets and their descriptions. 

1) day.csv: This dataset tracks the daily bike share count and weather characteristics from 2011 to 2012 in the Capital bikeshare system in the Washington, DC metro area. 

2) days_with_weather.csv: This dataset tracks is the same data as day.csv with the addition of maximum temperature, average windspeed, and precipitation. 

3) hour.csv: This was a dataset of hourly usage of the Capital Bikeshare system from 2011 through 2012.

We augmented these datasets with additional datasets discussed in the Other Resources folder. Our final analysis is performed on the combined dataset in said folder.
