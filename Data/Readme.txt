=========================
DATA STRUCTURE/REPOSITORY
=========================

The Data folder contains datasets used in our analysis. Below are our datasets and their descriptions. 

1) day.csv: This dataset tracks the daily bike share count and weather characteristics from 2011 to 2012 in the Capital bikeshare system in the Washington, DC metro area. 

2) days_with_weather.csv: This dataset tracks is the same data as day.csv with the addition of maximum temperature, average windspeed, and precipitation.
